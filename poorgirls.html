MARRIAGE FOR POOR GIRLS

At Manav Kalyan Foundation, we believe every girl deserves a bright future, free from exploitation and poverty. Our "Marriage for Poor Girls" project tackles the devastating issue of child marriage, specifically targeting girls from underprivileged backgrounds. Child marriage robs young girls of their childhood, education, and health. Through "Marriage for Poor Girls," we offer a comprehensive approach: Education & Empowerment: We provide educational opportunities for girls at risk of child marriage. Education equips them with skills and knowledge to build independent lives. Family Support: We work with families to address the root causes of child marriage, such as financial hardship. We offer alternative income generation opportunities and raise awareness of the negative consequences of child marriage. Community Advocacy: We collaborate with local leaders and organizations to shift societal norms and create a supportive environment that values girls' education and well-being. By empowering young girls and their families, we break the cycle of poverty and child marriage. Your support for "Marriage for Poor Girls" can transform lives. Donate today and help us create a future where every girl has the chance to reach her full potential.
