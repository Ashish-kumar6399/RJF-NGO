Frequently asked questions

Is MANAV KALYAN FOUNDATION an NGO?
Yes, MANAV KALYAN FOUNDATION is an NGO (registered as charitable trust in year 2011)
Does MANAV KALYAN FOUNDATION has 80G number?
Yes. MANAV KALYAN FOUNDATION has 80G number, all doners get copy of 80G along with receipt and eligible of IT tax rebate under section 80G.
Does MANAV KALYAN FOUNDATION is eligible to take donations from foreign countries?
Yes, MANAV KALYAN FOUNDATION has FCRA No and eligible to accept donations from outside India.
Do MANAV KALYAN FOUNDATION work only for special children?
No MANAV KALYAN FOUNDATION has a vast and diverse area of working, it works with children and adults of special needs as well as with mothers of special children. Our livelihood program includes marginalized rural women groups also along with PWDs Another area of work is mental health awareness, fighting the taboo, treatment, psychological behavioural assessment, psychological counselling, guidance, suicide prevention program.

Difference between mental retardation and mental illness?
Mental retardation is a permanent condition in which the development of the brain stopped at a certain age or can be appeared by birth. Mental illness is a mental disease that can appear at any age of life and it is treatable.
