Why Mithila culture ?

Mithila culture is a rich heritage known for its vibrant Madhubani paintings, Maithili literature, and deep-rooted traditions. Home to great scholars like Vidyapati and Raja Janak, it has been a center of intellectual and artistic excellence. Preserving this culture strengthens community bonds, safeguards traditional knowledge, and keeps its timeless legacy alive for future generations.

Its unique festivals, folk music, and craftsmanship reflect the region's deep cultural roots and artistic brilliance. Mithila’s traditional values promote social harmony, spirituality, and respect for nature. Reviving and promoting Mithila culture creates economic opportunities for artisans and preserves its artistic identity. Embracing Mithila’s heritage ensures that its wisdom and creativity continue to inspire the world.

WHAT WE DO

Our NGO is dedicated to preserving, promoting, and revitalizing the rich heritage of Mithila culture. We work to safeguard traditional art forms like Madhubani painting, Maithili literature, folk music, and dance by supporting local artisans and providing them with training, resources, and market opportunities. Through cultural festivals, workshops, and awareness programs, we aim to educate people about Mithila’s unique traditions and their significance in modern society.
